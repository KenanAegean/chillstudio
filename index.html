02 — The Mechanic

Unique
Echolocation

Trapped in total darkness, your only hope is a device that translates sound into sight. Every scan reveals the world around you, shaping paths out of the void, but in this distorted mirror world, time your scans carefully, or risk exposing yourself.

  • — Navigate by Echolocation: Use a unique echolocation device to map your surroundings. Every scan provides a brief moment of clarity in a world of absolute shadow.
  • — The Cost of Sight: Every pulse is a signal. Balance the desperate need to see with the necessity of silence, timing your scans carefully to avoid revealing your location to the things lurking in the dark.
  • — Something is Listening: Stella, a corrupted reflection of the past, is drawn to your signals. She is hunting you, determined to keep you in her world forever.
  • — Uncover Your Identity: Explore the environment to find shattered mirror shards. Piece them together to reveal the truth of who you are and why you’re here.
